About us

A growing family of local people learning to follow Jesus and live out our lives for the benefit of our community.

Sunday gatherings

We meet at the Whitemoor Centre on John O'Gaunts Way (map) on the 1st, 2nd and 4th Sundays of the month.

The 3rd (and sometimes 5th) Sundays are kept aside to allow time to spend with our families, neighbours and friends.

Gatherings start at 10:30am.

During the week

We want people to find a place within BCC where they feel comfortable.

We have a range of small groups, learning programmes and missional communities, each with their own character.

Talk to one of the leadership team to see where you might best fit.
